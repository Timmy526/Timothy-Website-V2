My Photography

I started photography in the 11th grade as a elective program. I never really thought that Photography was for me, and frankly I thought I wasn't that good at taking photos. But slowly through the class, my teacher told me that I was a great photographer and that I should continue to take photos. So I bought my own camera and have continued to take photos ever since. My main goal of photography is to show the beauty of the world, that we continue to walk past and never stop to truely enjoy.

    My Photography

    My Story: I started photography in the fall of 2017 as an elective program. I started learning at a point of my life where I didn't think I was good at art in anyway. However, I didnt know that photography was a form of art, and that I would be able to capture many great things that go on in this world. In that photography elective, my teacher taught me how to take good photos and even showed off some of my photos that I took for weekend homework. My teacher told me I have a good eye for my photos and that it is something I should continue doing. Ever since that day, I created an instagram account and bought my own camera to take photos.

    My Mission: To show the beauty of the world, that we continue to walk past and never stop to truely enjoy.
